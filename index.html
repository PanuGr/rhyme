    About this app

    This app uses the Datamuse API to help you find words for your poetry or creative writing, enhanced with AI capabilities to suggest poem ideas, provide structure analysis, and help you complete your work.

    Simply enter a word, choose how you want to find related words, and click on any result to add it to your text. Then use the AI tools to enhance your creativity.
